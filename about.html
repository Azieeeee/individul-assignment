ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Nur Azieyana Binti Pazil, a web designer with over 5 years of experience creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design. My passion for design and attention to detail help me bring unique and functional websites to life.

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Information Management from Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM), Machang branch, where I developed a strong foundation in both technical and creative aspects of web development. Throughout my career, I've worked with various clients, ranging from small businesses to large corporations, delivering high-quality designs that meet their needs.

My key skills include:

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Responsive Design
  • UI/UX Design
  • Graphic Design
  • SEO Optimization

I am committed to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest trends and technologies in web design. My goal is to create websites that not only look great but also provide an excellent user experience.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy photography, hiking, and exploring new design tools and techniques.
